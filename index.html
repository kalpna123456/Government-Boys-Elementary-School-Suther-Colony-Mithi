Latest News



Children's Day

Children’s Day in Pakistan is celebrated every year on 20th November, which is also observed as Universal Children’s Day around the world.



Culture Day

Culture Day is a day to celebrate our beautiful traditions and customs. On this day, students wear colorful traditional clothes and take part in songs, dances, and plays that show our culture. It is a fun day.



Festivals

Diwali is one of the most popular festivals celebrated in India. It is also called the Festival of Lights. People decorate their homes with diyas, candles, and colorful lights.



Science Lab

The lab helps us understand the topics we study in books. It is clean, bright, and safe for students. I love spending time in the science lab because we can see and learn science in real life.



Games

Games play a very important role in a student’s life. They help us stay healthy, active, and strong. Playing games teaches us teamwork, discipline, and honesty.



Independent Day

Independence Day is celebrated every year on 14th August in Pakistan. It is a very important day in the history of our country. On this day in 1947, Pakistan got freedom from British rule.
